What is SPARK?

More than just a place to hang out.

On its face, SPARK is a cool, fun place for teens to hang out after school. Skateboarding, art, swimming, music, mountain biking — there's always something happening.

But if you look a little closer, you'll find something more. SPARK is a place where we believe that it's the relationships that matter most. Every kid who steps through our door deserves to know, beyond a doubt, that people here care about them.

SPARK is kindness. SPARK is compassion. SPARK is where you belong.

For Families

Strengthening Families Workshops

SPARK offers the Strengthening Families program for families with children ages 7–17. These are hands-on, practical sessions — not lectures. Parents leave with real skills they can use.

Childcare may be available. Snacks and a light meal are provided each session. Stipends available for participants.

Questions? Contact Scott or Liesl at 715.600.3001
